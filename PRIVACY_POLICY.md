# Privacy Policy

PluckVocab does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Stored

When you pluck a word, the following data is saved locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`:

- The selected word
- The URL and title of the page where the word was plucked
- The timestamp of the action

## Data Sharing

No data is sent to any external server. All data remains on your device.

## Data Deletion

You can clear all stored data at any time by removing the extension.

## Contact

If you have questions, please open an issue at https://github.com/KinjiKawaguchi/PluckVocab/issues.
